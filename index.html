In this space, we slow down. We get curious. We return to presence. We re-learn what it means to truly see and hear each other. We re-learn how to meet others from the heart, not the head — and how to date in a way that feels aligned, nourishing, and real.

We do it through relational games and practices

As well as a continuous group discussion that invites your voice in -

The same way you would bring your voice into a relationship.

This course is free from the need to change or fix anything.

There are no formulas, checklists, or step by step guides.

Rather, it is a highly interactive, experiential learning and practicing environment rich in loving supportive feedback

We learn through experience because it is the experience that embeds this work deeply within us and allows us to connect from a very different place organically.

In this course, we’ll explore:

  • Ways to reconnect to our wholeness and a sense of being/doing totally enough in relationships
  • The ability to tap into our bodily wisdom for guidance, discernment, and clarity when navigating relational situations
  • The invaluable skills of how to use vulnerability timely and respectfully to create emotional intimacy and instant connection.
  • How to look at connection from the place of personal power.
  • The language to articulate the whole richness of our authentic experience - emotions, sensations, and intentions - for deeper, more intimate conversations
  • The art of receiving another - making another feel deeply seen, heard, and gotten inside.
  • Practical ways of approaching others with ease, authenticity, and genuine bold curiosity
  • Tools to deal with the discomfort of rejection and how to keep ourselves open to love in the face of emotional pain.
  • How to relate in the face of differences in our relationships
  • Language to transform conflict and communicate emotional charge to release relational anxiety and resentment.
